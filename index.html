MindSpark's Campus Ambassador Program is a program which shapes students from various colleges across the nation to become efficient managers and creative leaders.

Ambassadors not only acquire leadership skills/entrepreneurial qualities during the program, but also gain social recognition.

We call for ambassadors to support their constituencies, and educate them about the powerful role played by technical festivals.

Why become A Campus Ambassador?

LEARN

Learn to associate with people. Understand and explore various technical and marketing issues and gain work experience.

BUILD

Build a network with other budding minds across the nation, by collaborating and sharing ideas through our platform.

IMPROVE

An opportunity to improve your communication and managerial skills by interacting with people coming from diverse fields and from various colleges across India.

INCENTIVES

Internship Opportunity

This will be given to the best ambassador,decided based on the amount of work done for MindSpark.

Events

Campus ambassador gets two free event registrations in MindSpark'20.

Certificate

An official certificate of appreciation will be given to all Campus Ambassadors.

Goodies and passes

There are a variety of coupons, vouchers and other exciting goodies to be won.Top campus ambassadors will get free passes of keynote lectures and pronite.

Accommodation

Accommodation will be provided to the top campus ambassadors during the technical festival, MindSpark.

RESPONSIBILITIES

PUBLICITY: Putting up posters of MindSpark'19 on college bulletins and notice boards within 2-3 days of receiving them.Ensuring faculty approval for MindSpark coordinators to do publicity in your college.

PARTICIPATION:Ensure keen participation of your College in various Events conducted by MindSpark, in COEP or in your College or City.

SHARE:Publicising the events of MindSpark by sharing the posts on various social media platforms like WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.Forwarding the mails related to MindSpark to the students of respective college
